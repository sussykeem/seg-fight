User Manual: SegFight

Karim Chmayssani, Blake Milstead, Turner Heath, Randy Lin

Unzip SegFight.zip into the directory of your choice.
To start the game, simply open the SegFight.exe, no compilation or special libraries will be needed
In order to play the game, you will need two Xbox or Playstation gamepads. The game will not work if two controllers
are not connected before the launch.

Controls:
	Movement/Menuing - Left Joystick/Dpad
	Select/Light Attack - South Button
	Heavy - East Button
	Special - North Button
	Guardbreak - West Button

Known bugs:
	Very high jump will occur sometimes
	Walk animation can occasionally be slightly off
